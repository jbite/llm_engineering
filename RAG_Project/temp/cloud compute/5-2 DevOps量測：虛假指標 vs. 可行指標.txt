# DevOps中實用與虛榮指標的重要性

歡迎閱讀我們的博客，探討在DevOps實踐中衡量指標的重要性，特別是區分實用指標和虛榮指標。

## 虛榮指標的局限性

- **虛榮指標的問題**: 虛榮指標，比如網站“點擊量”，雖然聽起來很不錯，但實際上對決策行動沒有幫助。它們無法告訴你應該采取什麼行動，因為它們不代表具體的用戶行為。

## 實用指標的價值

- **實用指標的重要性**: 與虛榮指標不同，實用指標可以幫助你做出決策。例如，通過A/B測試新功能，並觀察收入變化，可以幫助你決定是否應該將該功能推廣到更多用戶。
- **實用指標範例**: 來自《精益創業》作者Eric Ries的實用指標包括縮短新功能的上市時間、提高產品可用性、減少部署時間、提前發現缺陷、更有效地利用硬件基礎設施和快速向產品經理反饋性能和用戶信息。

## DevOps行動指標

- **Nicole Forsgren的觀點**: 在她2017年的演講中，Nicole Forsgren確定了她認為的四大DevOps實用指標：平均引導時間、發布頻率、變更失敗率和平均恢復時間。
- **實用指標的應用**: 這些指標有助於你衡量和改進你的流程，並朝著目標前進。

在這篇博客文章中，您了解到虛榮指標可能一開始看起來很吸引人，但提供的實際行動洞見有限。實用指標提供了衡量過程和實現目標的有意義方法。DevOps的實用指標包括平均引導時間、發布頻率、變更失敗率和平均恢復時間。

# The Importance of Actionable and Vanity Metrics in DevOps

Welcome to our blog exploring the significance of measuring the right metrics in DevOps practices, distinguishing between actionable and vanity metrics.

## Limitations of Vanity Metrics

- **Issue with Vanity Metrics**: Vanity metrics, such as website "hits," might sound impressive but are ineffective for actionable decisions. They fail to represent specific user behaviors or guide you on what actions to take next.

## Value of Actionable Metrics

- **Importance of Actionable Metrics**: Unlike vanity metrics, actionable metrics help you make decisions. For instance, introducing a new feature via A/B testing and observing revenue changes can guide whether to expand the feature to more users.
- **Examples of Actionable Metrics**: Actionable metrics suggested by Eric Ries, author of "The Lean Startup," include reducing time to market for new features, improving product availability, reducing deployment time, detecting defects earlier, efficient use of hardware infrastructure, and quickly providing feedback to product managers.

## Actionable Metrics in DevOps

- **Nicole Forsgren's Insights**: In her 2017 talk, Nicole Forsgren identified the top four actionable metrics in DevOps: mean lead time, release frequency, change failure rate, and mean time to recovery.
- **Applying Actionable Metrics**: These metrics help you measure and improve your processes and work towards your goals.

In this blog post, you learned that vanity metrics may be initially appealing but offer limited actionable insights. Actionable metrics provide meaningful ways to measure your processes and work toward goals. Key DevOps actionable metrics include mean lead time, release frequency, change failure rate, and mean time to recovery.
